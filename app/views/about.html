To broaden your horizons

Be prepared

For anything

It's important to do your research in advance and to be prepared for anything that might come up. It's also helpful to have a good guide or itinerary so that you can make the most of your time there. Finally, It's important to be flexible and open-minded, as there will likely be things that you hadn't planned for or that won't go exactly as you'd hoped. Ultimately, as long as you're willing to go with the flow and adapt as needed, then you'll be just fine!

Traveling

With kids

It's important to choose a destination that's kid-friendly and that offers lots of fun and engaging activities for kids. It's helpful to get an understanding of the local customs and etiquette before you go, so that you can be sure to avoid anything that might cause offense or upset to the locals.
